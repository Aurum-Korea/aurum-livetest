§ 04
Partners console
/admin · 파트너 전용
Where the three of you triage every inquiry. Six representative leads are seeded: a fresh inquiry, one in review with a partner note, an approved patron with NDA pending upload, an approved patron with NDA awaiting your review (auto-selected on load — the most actionable state), an approved patron with full archive history including a rejection-then-approval cycle, and one declined inquiry. The EXPORT NDAs button in the top nav downloads a CSV manifest of every uploaded NDA across all leads, for legal/compliance archiving.

END-TO-END · 전체 동선
/interest form → partner approves in /admin → email lands in inbox → /code (auto-validates) → /nda upload → partner approves NDA in /admin → /memo opens
What's mocked vs. what's real
  • The code page form click does nothing in preview (the real one validates against the server and forwards on)
  • The NDA page is frozen in the "awaiting review" state for inspection. A live patron would see this state until a partner approves their upload, at which point the page auto-updates within 30 seconds
  • The memo doc-card "OPEN" buttons would normally stream the actual PDFs through a gated server endpoint; in preview they'll 404 since the server isn't running
  • The admin dashboard's six leads are fictional but cover every state your real dashboard will show: NEW, REVIEWING, SENT/awaiting NDA, SENT/NDA awaiting review, SENT/NDA approved with archive history, DECLINED
  • All mutating actions in the preview (approve, reject, send email, etc.) are blocked — preview is read-only
